Cherry-Topped Cheesecake

Description

Cheesecake with a rich, creamy texture and topped with cherries.

Ingredients

  • 1 and 3/4 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
  • 1 and 1/4 cups sugar, divided
  • 3 (8 oz) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 (21 oz.) can cherry pie filling (optional)

Steps

  1. Make the crust and press into a springform pan.
  2. Make the filling, then pour it over the crust.
  3. Bake the cheesecake until teh filling is almost set.
  4. Run a knife around teh rim to loosen the sides and allow it to cool.
  5. Remove the cheesecake from the pan and chill before serving.
